My Journey

My journey as an IT programmer started with a passion for problem-solving and a love of technology. I began my journey by studying computer science in college, where I learned the fundamentals of programming languages such as Java, C++, and Python. After graduating, I landed my first job as a junior software developer at a tech startup. Here, I was exposed to agile software development methodologies and gained valuable experience in developing web and mobile applications using modern frameworks like React and Angular. As I gained more experience, I became interested in the field of artificial intelligence and machine learning. I attended workshops and online courses to learn more about these exciting areas of technology and eventually became proficient in developing AI-powered solutions.
